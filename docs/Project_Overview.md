1. Product Summary

This project is a web-based free video streaming platform focused on creator-driven content and viewer engagement. The initial phase prioritizes a fast, reliable, and scalable MVP that enables creators to upload videos and viewers to browse, follow creators, and stream content smoothly worldwide.

There is no payment or monetization system in Phase 1. The platform focuses entirely on content delivery, streaming performance, and a solid technical foundation for future expansion.

2. User Roles

Creators:
- Upload and manage video content
- Manage video metadata (title, description, visibility)
- View basic engagement metrics

Viewers:
- Browse video content
- Follow creators
- Stream videos globally with minimal buffering

3. Core MVP Features (Phase 1)

- User authentication and profiles
- Creator video upload
- Video listing and browsing
- Video streaming playback
- Follow creator functionality
- Basic engagement tracking (views)


4. Non-Goals (Phase 1)

- No payment or subscription system
- No advanced recommendation algorithms
- No live streaming
- No advanced content moderation tools

5. Key Technical Priorities

- Smooth video playback worldwide
- Scalable and maintainable architecture
- CDN-backed video delivery
MVP-first development approach
